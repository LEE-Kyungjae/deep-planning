# Palamedes Landing Copy

## Hero

Palamedes helps builders working with AI choose a direction before execution hardens the wrong path.

Track one current plan, the evidence behind it, the signals that would invalidate it, and the exact moment to replan.

## Short Version

Palamedes is a local planning engine for builders who can ship fast but do not want to drift.

It keeps planning state in your repo so you can:

- choose one direction
- reject weak directions early
- attach evidence and hypotheses to the plan
- review changes over time
- restore a better prior direction when needed

## Problem

AI made execution cheaper.
It did not make direction easier.

Teams can generate code, tasks, and workflows faster than ever, but still struggle with:

- what to build first
- why this path is better than the alternatives
- what evidence supports the choice
- what failure signal means the plan should change

## Positioning

Palamedes is not:

- a note-taking app
- a generic PM tool
- a workflow engine
- an execution agent runtime

Palamedes is:

- a versioned planning state
- an evidence-backed decision loop
- a local system for review, replan, and restore

## Outcome

After one Palamedes loop, you should have:

- one chosen direction
- the directions you rejected
- a success metric and deadline
- explicit failure signals
- the next evidence to collect
- a clear replan point

## Who It Is For

- solo builders using AI to explore product ideas
- founders trying to hold direction steady while shipping fast
- 0->1 teams running repeated experiments

## Taglines

- Decide what matters before AI builds it.
- Version your direction, not just your code.
- Keep plan state as strong as execution speed.
- Replan with evidence, not vibes.
